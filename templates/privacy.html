Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use Spendly, we store the expense records you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Spendly service: displaying your expense history, generating summaries, and allowing you to manage your records. We do not use your data for advertising or sell it to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical measures to protect it from unauthorised access. You may request a copy or deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly uses Google Fonts to load typefaces. This causes your browser to make a request to Google's servers, which may log your IP address per Google's own privacy policy. We do not integrate with any payment processors or analytics services that collect personal data.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, or wish to exercise your data rights, please contact us via the details provided on the site.
